About

The project is a comprehensive land registration platform that streamlines the process of buying, selling, and managing properties. It allows users to securely register land, check and verify legal documents, and compare market prices in real-time. The platform provides detailed property maps, aiding both individual and industrial buyers in making informed decisions. Users can download verified documents directly from the system, ensuring all legalities are covered. Whether it's local residential plots or large industrial sites, the platform caters to all types of land transactions, offering a transparent and efficient solution for all stakeholders in the realestate market.
